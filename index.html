Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my account?
To activate your account, you need to deposit at least 100 ETB. After signing up, you'll receive a 300 ETB bonus. Once you make your first deposit, your account will be fully activated and you can start investing in digital instruments.
How does the bonus system work?
Our bonus system provides multipliers based on your deposit amount:

- 100 ETB deposit → 1x bonus (100 ETB total bonus)
- 200 ETB deposit → 2x bonus (400 ETB total bonus)
- 300 ETB deposit → 4x bonus (1,200 ETB total bonus)
- 600 ETB deposit → 6x bonus (3,600 ETB total bonus)
- 1,000 ETB deposit → 10x bonus (10,000 ETB total bonus)

Bonuses are added to your account immediately after deposit verification.
How do digital instruments work?
Digital instruments are investment tools that generate income over a specific duration. Each instrument has a fixed price and income potential. After purchase, you'll start earning returns over the specified duration (ranging from 30 to 90 days). Earnings are credited to your account on a weekly basis and can be withdrawn at any time.
How do I withdraw my earnings?
To withdraw your earnings, go to the Wallet section and click on "Withdraw Funds". Select your preferred withdrawal method (CBE, Awash Bank, or Telebirr), enter the amount and your account details. Withdrawal requests are processed within 72 hours, and the funds will be transferred to your specified account.
Is Meta X secure?
Yes, Meta X prioritizes security. We use two-factor authentication (2FA) for all transactions, ensuring your funds and account information remain secure. All data is encrypted, and our platform is regularly updated with the latest security measures. Additionally, Meta X is backed by Meta, providing an extra layer of trust and reliability.
